The Importance of Building Financial Foundations Early

Teaching money management to elementary and junior high school students is not just about numbers, but about forming discipline and independence. During this transition, students begin to understand the value of effort and patience in achieving goals through savings. Financial literacy instilled early will be a valuable provision for them in making wise financial decisions in the future.

Through the simulation program at Bank Devisa Nusantara, students are invited to experience real banking experiences but within a safe school environment. Saving is no longer just storing money in a piggy bank, but a learning process to understand balance systems, mutations, and structured financial target planning.

This program is specifically designed to support the school curriculum in creating a financially intelligent generation. With an interactive digital approach, elementary students will feel happy, while junior high students will feel more responsible for managing their own funds.
